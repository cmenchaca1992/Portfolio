For my capstone project I will be working on an app idea called Tendies (name is still in the air). The basis of this app is like Tender the dating app but for food that you pair with your significant other or a group of friends. After pairing with them you proceed to swipe right or left on food catagories and then specific restaurants to see where to eat. (I also do not have an icon idea yet so this is just a place holder)
